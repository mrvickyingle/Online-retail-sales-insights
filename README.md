# Online Retail Sales Analysis Dashboard (Microsoft Excel)

## Overview

This project is an end-to-end sales analysis performed entirely in **Microsoft Excel** using an online retail dataset. The objective was to clean raw transactional data, prepare analytical datasets, build interactive dashboards, and communicate meaningful business insights.

The project demonstrates a complete Excel-based analytics workflow, including:

- Data cleaning using **Power Query**
- Data transformation and preparation
- Pivot Tables and Pivot Charts
- Interactive Dashboard
- Slicers and Timeline filters
- Business insight generation and reporting

---

# Business Objective

The goal of this project is to analyze retail sales performance and answer key business questions such as:

- How much revenue has the business generated?
- Which countries contribute the most sales?
- Which products generate the highest revenue?
- What are the seasonal sales trends?
- How do domestic and international sales compare?
- What insights can help improve business decision-making?

---

# Dataset Description

The project uses an **Online Retail** transactional dataset containing customer purchases from multiple countries.

The dataset includes information such as:

- Invoice Number
- Product Code
- Product Description
- Quantity
- Invoice Date
- Unit Price
- Customer ID
- Country

Additional calculated fields were created during data preparation for better analysis.

# Key Insights

### 1. Revenue Performance

- Total revenue generated is **$10.6 million**.
- The dataset contains **19,960 invoices** with an average invoice value of **$533.17**.

---

### 2. Geographic Distribution

- Sales were recorded across **38 countries**.
- Domestic sales account for **90%** of total revenue.
- International sales contribute the remaining **10%**.

---

### 3. Seasonal Trend

- Sales increase significantly between **September and November**.
- These months represent the strongest sales period in the dataset.

> **Note:** The available data for **December 2011 may be incomplete**. Any decline observed during this month should therefore be interpreted with caution and should not be treated as evidence of reduced business performance.

---

### 4. Product Performance

The highest revenue-generating products are:

- DOTCOM POSTAGE
- REGENCY CAKESTAND 3 TIER
- PAPER CRAFT LITTLE BIRDIE

Together, these three products contribute approximately **47% of total sales**, indicating a strong concentration of revenue among a small number of products.
